As the copywriter intern on a 5-person creative team, I helped concept and create the Minnesta Wild 2017-18 NHL season campaign.


We invited fans to bring water from their lakes, ponds, and backyard rinks, and add it to the ice surface at the Xcel Energy Center.


The campaign launched before the season with an event to collect water donations.




We made an interactive digital map for fans to share where their water came from, and what that ice surface means to them.









Rabbit from Super Troopers yelled the line during a Wild game. He got the word "meow" in there once.


The campaign won a 2018 Grand Sports Clio, One Show Silver Pencil, Gold National Addy, and more. The Wild broke their previous record for ticket sales, and made the playoffs.
